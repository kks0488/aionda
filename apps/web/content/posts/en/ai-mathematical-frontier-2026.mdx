## FAQ

**Q: Will AI completely replace human mathematicians?**
A: It is an expansion rather than a replacement. AI plays the role of an 'explorer' searching through a vast logical search space, while humans take on the role of 'strategists' who determine the destination of that exploration and assign meaning to the discovered results. Mathematics remains a domain that requires human creativity.

**Q: How does this technology benefit general users?**
A: AI with enhanced mathematical reasoning capabilities also sees a dramatic increase in everyday logical problem-solving. It is equivalent to having an error-free assistant for complex tax calculations, reviewing legal clauses, or establishing optimized business strategies.

**Q: Are there risks in terms of security or ethics?**
A: Mathematically perfect reasoning capabilities could potentially become powerful tools for decryption. For this reason, research institutions are simultaneously conducting 'alignment' technology research to control AI's mathematical capabilities and guide them in a safe direction.

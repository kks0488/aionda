## TL;DR
- YouTube plans to introduce AI Digital Twin technology for creators on Shorts.
- The feature aims to improve production efficiency by reducing physical creation constraints.
- YouTube will update rights authentication systems and safety guidelines for AI content.

## Current Status
YouTube is preparing a feature for creators to make Shorts using digital clones. This technology learns physical characteristics and voice data to generate a resembling avatar. Creators can produce short videos using avatars via text or simple controls.

The feature focuses on automating the production process. Digital twins can reduce the need for lighting, backgrounds, and costumes. YouTube plans to update rights authentication and safety guidelines for generative AI content. Specific details like release schedules and supported languages are not yet officially announced.

"Are you trying to create a video with Sora 2, but it's not working?" (Problem)
As of January 10, 2026, **OpenAI has officially discontinued the Free Tier video generation feature for Sora 2**. Video generation has now become the **exclusive privilege of Plus or Pro subscribers** paying over $20 per month. (Solution)
Behind this sudden policy change lies the inevitable choice to handle exploding GPU costs and maintain high-quality service. We have summarized the details of this policy change and strategies for different users. (Evidence)

## ðŸ’¸ What Changed? (Policy Update)

### 1. Free Tier Access Blocked
- **Previous**: Sora 2 video generation possible within limited counts.
- **New**: Video generation **impossible**. (Image generation may also be partially restricted)
- **Reason**: The rendering cost of Sora 2 is tens of times higher than text models (GPT-5), making it difficult to sustain free traffic.

### 2. Enhanced Benefits for Plus / Pro Subscribers
Paid users receive a faster and more stable Priority Queue.
- **Plus ($20/mo)**: Standard speed, daily generation limits apply.
- **Pro ($200/mo)**: High-speed mode, commercial usage rights, higher generation limits.

## Why Now? (Background)

Sora 2 shocked the world with Hollywood-level video quality upon release, but it was also criticized as an **"energy-eating monster."**
As the user base recently skyrocketed, server load reached a critical point, and OpenAI pressed the button to end free use under the pretext of **"maintaining service quality."** This is similar to the restrictions during the early days of DALL-E 3, but much more radical.

## ðŸ‘‰ Action Guide for Users

### 1. Casual Users
If you created videos occasionally for fun, a $20 monthly fee might be burdensome.
- **Alternative**: Try the free tiers of **Runway Gen-3 Alpha** or **Luma Dream Machine**. Although restricted, they still allow free usage.

### 2. Creators and Developers (Pro/Dev)
If you use Sora 2 for work, a paid subscription is essential.
- **API Users**: API policies have also changed. Calls via API are billed separately, so if you integrated Sora 2 into existing apps, beware of **cost bombs**. Be sure to set a `usage limit`.

## FAQ: Questions About Sora 2 Pricing

### Q1. Will my previously generated free videos be deleted?
**A.** No, your existing videos will be preserved. However, features like 'Generate More' or 'Remix' will be locked until you switch to a paid subscription.

### Q2. Will GPT-5 text chat also become paid-only?
**A.** Currently, this measure is limited to the video (Sora 2) generation feature. Text models like GPT-5.1 / 5.2 are still available (with limits) on the free tier.

### Q3. Are there student discounts?
**A.** There are no official student discounts currently. Access may be possible only through school accounts that have adopted the ChatGPT Edu plan.

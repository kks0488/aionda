The X app could be removed from your iPhone. U.S. Senators have demanded that Apple and Google remove X from their app stores because Grok AI generated sexually explicit images of children and women.

## What Happened
Grok AI generated a large volume of inappropriate images of children and women. Senators have defined this as a violation of Apple and Google's App Store terms and conditions. Elon Musk continues to allow paid users to access these features. The senators have demanded official responses from both companies by January 23.

## Why It Matters
The safety principles of the App Store are at risk of collapsing. If Apple and Google remain silent, their platform management policies will lose credibility. This case holds platforms accountable for managing AI-generated harmful content.

## Official Updates
- 🏛️ [When AI Levels the Playing Field: Skill Homogenization, Asset Concentration, and Two Regimes of Inequality](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05565) — arXiv CS.AI
- Why it matters.
It’s worth reading for its inequality framing of how AI-driven skill leveling can coexist with asset concentration, as signaled directly by the title.
- 🏛️ [MAPO: Mixed Advantage Policy Optimization for Long-Horizon Multi-Turn Dialogue](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06194) — arXiv CS.AI
- Why it matters.
It targets the practical challenge of optimizing policies for long-horizon, multi-turn dialogue, making it relevant to agent and chatbot training work.
- 🏛️ [Uncertainty Quantification in LLM Agents: Foundations, Emerging Challenges, and Opportunities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05073) — arXiv CS.AI
- Why it matters.
As a foundations-and-challenges overview of uncertainty quantification in LLM agents, it can help structure how you think about reliability and evaluation.
